Overview

This website is a curated platform hosting teaching materials, reproducible workflows, datasets, and analysis tools for forest remote sensing and geospatial analytics. The content integrates R, LiDAR processing, and cloud-based platforms such as Google Earth Engine (GEE) to support both research and education.

The goal is to make advanced remote sensing methods accessible and reproducible. Materials are organized to help students and researchers follow complete processing pipelines—from data access and preparation to mapping, modeling, evaluation, and exporting final products.

Rather than a personal webpage, this site functions as a shared learning and research environment where code, tutorials, and applications are continuously expanded and documented over time.
