About

This site is a curated hub for R and Google Earth Engine (GEE) code, processing workflows, and companion materials supporting my research and teaching. The structure is designed to be simple to navigate and easy to extend over time.

The teaching materials and workflows presented here aim to lower the barrier to entry for remote sensing and forest analytics, particularly for students and researchers working with LiDAR and cloud platforms such as Google Earth Engine. While independent, these resources are conceptually consistent with broader efforts to improve accessibility, reproducibility, and global collaboration in forest monitoring.
