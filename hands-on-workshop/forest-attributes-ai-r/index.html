Mapping of forest attributes using airborne laser scanning (ALS) and artificial intelligence (AI) workshop

ForestSAT2026 Workshop Series

Workshop aim and purpose

This hands-on workshop equips participants with the knowledge and tools to access, handle, process, generate, and interpret forest attribute maps from ALS datasets. All work is conducted in R. Through guided exercises, participants learn to use ALS data, integrate field measurements, and create maps of forest attributes, including aboveground biomass and carbon stock, gaining insight into best practices and methodological limitations.
